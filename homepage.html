Our Story

Ernie and Sylvester met in high school and became fast friends, bonded by their love of food and their entrepreneurial spirit. As they grew older, they both pursued careers in the food industry, but they never forgot their dream of starting their own business.

Years later, when they were both retired, they decided to turn their passion for food into a reality. They decided to open a bagel shop in their hometown of Medford, Massachusetts. They wanted to create a warm and welcoming space where people could gather, chat, and enjoy a delicious bagel. They spent months perfecting their recipes, sourcing the best ingredients, and creating a menu that was both classic and innovative.

On opening day, the line of customers extended out the door, and the shop quickly became a staple in the community. Ernie and Sylvester were thrilled to see how much their customers enjoyed their bagels, and they worked tirelessly to ensure that every bagel was made with care and precision. As the years went by, Ernie and Sylvester's Bagel Haven became a beloved institution in Medford, known for its friendly service, fresh ingredients, and delicious bagels.

The two best friends continued to work together every day, making sure that every customer who walked through the door felt like family. It is why they get to the store at 2am every day and back fresh bagels every two hours, so that their bagels are fresh and delicious for their customers to enjoy by morning rush hour.

Ernie and Sylvester's Bagel Haven is more than just a business; it's a testament to the friendship and passion that started it all. They are proud to have created a place where people can come together to enjoy good food and good company, and they look forward to serving their community for many years to come. There is no better way than with a bagel from E&S Bagels.
